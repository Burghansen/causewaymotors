What we do

Our services

From a 45-minute WOF to a full engine rebuild — one family-run Auckland workshop covering every job your vehicle will ever need.

High-quality workmanship

At a fair price.

Every job is quoted upfront and backed by our 12-month workmanship guarantee. If it isn't right, we make it right.

WOF Inspection

While-you-wait Warrant of Fitness covering brakes, tyres, lights, steering, structure, and glazing.

Full Servicing

Manufacturer-spec servicing with premium oils, filters, and fluids — keeps warranties valid and engines smooth.

Computer Diagnostics

OBD-II fault scans across engine, gearbox, ABS, airbags — plain-English reports, no jargon.

Brakes & Suspension

Pads, rotors, struts, shocks — precision handling and stopping power, restored.

Transmission & Clutch

Manual, automatic, CVT — diagnosis, fluid service, clutch replacement, and full rebuilds.

Engine Tuning & Carbon Clean

Specialist engine tuning plus carbon cleaning for direct-injection petrol and diesel engines.

Tyres & Wheel Alignment

New tyres, puncture repair, wheel balancing, and laser wheel alignment.

Air Conditioning

A/C re-gas, leak detection, and full climate system servicing for every make.

Panelbeating & Painting

Accident repair, panel replacement, and colour-matched respray — returned like new.
